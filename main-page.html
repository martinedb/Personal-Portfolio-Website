About Me

Hello! I’m Martin Edwini-Bonsu, a 5th-year Chemical and Biological Engineering student at the University of British Columbia with a passion for sustainability, clean water systems, and pollution control.

I’m currently completing a 16-month co-op with Associated Engineering, where I work on water and wastewater treatment design projects that range from chemical dosing systems to lagoon upgrades and energy audits.

I have a diverse range of work experiences. I’ve worked with sustainability-focused nonprofits and innovative startups to build career resources, manage digital platforms, develop tools like carbon footprint calculators, and create collaborative research databases for climate science.

When I’m not working, I enjoy cross-country running, learning about personal finance, cycling, working out at the gym, and creative writing (e.g. poetry, articles, and short stories). I bring curiosity, systems thinking, and people-centered design to everything I do.
